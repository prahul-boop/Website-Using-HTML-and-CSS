Membership Policy

  1. Only one membership per person is permitted. If we find that a member has multiple membership accounts, we reserve the right to cancel all memberships without refund.
  2. Memberships are non-refundable.
  3. Memberships may not be sold and/or transferred.
  4. Membership benefits are subject to change at any time.
  5. Members are responsible for updating their contact information and mailing address in their DummyKnows member profile and address list.
  6. Analog Members are responsible for redeeming their Membership Item before their Membership Item Code expires on February 1st of the following year (so the last day to use it is January 31st).
  7. If we determine a membership has been purchased for the sole purpose of purchasing and reselling tickets, DummyKnows reserves the right to cancel the ticket order and membership without refund.
  8. DummyKnows Pizza reserves the right to terminate forum posting privileges to any members not following the Forum Rules and Posting Guidelines.
  9. DummyKnows Pizza reserves the right to refuse service without refund to any members breaking rules or abusing their membership privileges.
